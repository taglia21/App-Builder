# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of LaunchForge services. By using our platform, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 1. Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use LaunchForge to:

### 1.1 Illegal Activities
- Generate applications that facilitate illegal activities in any jurisdiction
- Create tools for fraud, money laundering, or financial crimes
- Build systems that violate export control or sanctions laws
- Produce content that infringes intellectual property rights

### 1.2 Harmful Content
- Create applications containing malware, viruses, or malicious code
- Generate phishing sites or credential harvesting tools
- Build systems designed to harass, stalk, or harm individuals
- Produce hate speech, discriminatory content, or violent extremism

### 1.3 Abuse & Spam
- Use automated systems to abuse our API or services
- Generate bulk spam applications or email-sending tools
- Overwhelm our systems with excessive requests
- Attempt to bypass rate limits or usage restrictions

### 1.4 Deceptive Practices
- Create fake or misleading applications that impersonate others
- Generate scam or fraudulent websites
- Build applications that deceive users about their purpose
- Misrepresent your identity or affiliation

### 1.5 Security Violations
- Attempt to access other users' accounts or data
- Probe, scan, or test our systems for vulnerabilities
- Interfere with the security or integrity of our services
- Distribute tools designed to attack computer systems

### 1.6 Adult & Explicit Content
- Generate pornographic or sexually explicit applications
- Create content involving minors in any inappropriate context
- Build platforms primarily for adult content distribution

## 2. Content Ownership & Licensing

### 2.1 Your Content
- You retain full ownership of applications you generate
- You are responsible for ensuring your content doesn't infringe third-party rights
- You grant us a limited license to process your content for service delivery

### 2.2 Generated Code
- Code generated by LaunchForge is yours to use commercially
- We do not claim ownership of your generated applications
- Standard open-source licenses apply to included libraries

### 2.3 Third-Party Components
- Generated apps may include open-source components
- You must comply with applicable open-source licenses
- License information is included in generated projects

## 3. Rate Limits & Fair Use

### 3.1 API Rate Limits

| Plan | App Generations | API Calls | Concurrent Jobs |
|------|-----------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Free | 1 per month | 100/day | 1 |
| Starter | 10 per month | 1,000/day | 2 |
| Pro | Unlimited | 10,000/day | 5 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom | Custom |

### 3.2 Fair Use Guidelines
- Do not automate bulk generation of applications
- Space out large operations to avoid system strain
- Contact us for volume use cases before implementation

### 3.3 Overage Handling
- We may throttle requests exceeding your plan limits
- Persistent overages may require plan upgrade
- We reserve the right to suspend accounts for egregious abuse

## 4. Account Responsibilities

### 4.1 Security
- Keep your credentials confidential
- Enable two-factor authentication when available
- Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access
- Do not share account access with unauthorized users

### 4.2 Accuracy
- Provide accurate registration information
- Keep your contact information up to date
- Do not create multiple accounts to circumvent limits

### 4.3 Compliance
- Ensure your use complies with applicable laws
- Obtain necessary licenses for your applications
- Comply with export restrictions and sanctions

## 5. Enforcement Actions

We may take the following actions for policy violations:

### 5.1 Warning
- First-time minor violations may result in a warning
- We'll explain the violation and request correction

### 5.2 Content Removal
- We may remove applications that violate this policy
- You will be notified of removals when possible

### 5.3 Account Suspension
- Accounts may be suspended for:
  - Multiple warnings
  - Serious violations
  - Illegal activities
  - Security threats

### 5.4 Account Termination
- Accounts will be terminated for:
  - Repeated policy violations
  - Egregious harmful activities
  - Legal requirements
  - Non-payment after grace period

### 5.5 Legal Action
- We may involve law enforcement for criminal activities
- We cooperate with valid legal requests
- We reserve all legal rights and remedies

## 6. Appeals Process

If you believe action was taken in error:

1. **Contact Us**: Email compliance@launchforge.dev within 30 days
2. **Provide Details**: Explain why you believe the action was incorrect
3. **Review**: We will review your case within 10 business days
4. **Decision**: We will communicate our final decision in writing

## 7. Reporting Violations

To report AUP violations:

- **Email**: abuse@launchforge.dev
- **Include**: Description, evidence, and affected content
- **Response**: We investigate all reports within 48 hours

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this AUP as needed. Changes will be communicated via:
- Email to registered users
- Notice on our website
- In-app notification

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 9. Contact

For questions about this policy:

- **Email**: legal@launchforge.dev
- **Support**: support@launchforge.dev
